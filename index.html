A TASTE OF PORTUGUESE

About Us

Casita Express is your gateway to an authentic Portuguese dining experience in the heart of Emmarentia, Johannesburg. Established a couple of years ago, our culinary journey began with a passion for delivering the true essence of Portuguese cuisine. In 2020, we embraced a new identity as Casita Express, carving our niche with a focus on quality and tradition.

Products & services

Portuguese Chicken

Prawns, Hake, and Rissoles

Pasteis de Nata

Calamari

Casita Express invites you on a culinary journey with our authentic Portuguese menu. Indulge in the savory delights of Portuguese Chicken, Prawns, Hake, and Rissoles. Treat your sweet tooth to the rich flavors of Pasteis de Nata. Experience the taste of the sea with our Calamari or savor the convenience of our Wraps. Additionally, explore the fusion of Portuguese and Italian influences with our Pizza and Pasta offerings. Each dish is thoughtfully crafted to deliver an unforgettable dining experience, capturing the essence of Portugal's culinary traditions.
